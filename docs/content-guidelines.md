### Pricing Models

#### Time & Materials (T&M)

- **Best For**: Evolving requirements, exploratory projects
- **Billing**: Hourly rates based on role
- **Payment**: Monthly based on tracked hours
- **Flexibility**: High - adjust scope as needed

#### Fixed Price

**Best For**: Well-defined scope, specific deliverables

- **Pricing**: Total project cost (15-20% markup over T&M estimate)
- **Payment**: Milestone-based
- **Changes**: Via change request process
- **Risk**: Shared - scope must be clear

#### Retainer / Dedicated Resource

- **Best For**: Ongoing work, dedicated capacity
- **Pricing**: Monthly fixed rate (10% discount on hourly)
- **Commitment**: 3-6 month minimum
- **Billing**: Monthly in advance
- **Flexibility**: Medium - within allocated hours

#### Milestone-Based

- **Best For**: Large projects with clear phases
- **Payment**: Per milestone (30-40% per milestone)
- **Schedule**: Agreed upfront
- **Changes**: Adjust future milestones

---

### Payment Terms

#### Small Projects (<AED 40,000)

- **30%** upfront (start)
- **50%** at major milestone
- **20%** at completion

#### Medium Projects (AED 40,000-120,000)

#### Large Projects (AED 120,000+)

- **Milestone-based** (4-6 milestones)

#### Retainers

- **Monthly billing** in advance
- **Unused hours**: Roll over up to 20%
- **Additional hours**: At standard rate
